Delete Your Account

Hapus akun PajakPas Anda dan semua data terkait secara permanen.

Permanently delete your PajakPas account and all associated data.

Hapus Akun Otomatis

Delete Account Automatically

Masuk dengan akun Anda untuk menghapus semua data secara langsung.

Sign in with your account to delete all your data instantly.

Atau hapus melalui aplikasi

Or delete via the app

1

Buka aplikasi PajakPas

Open the PajakPas app

Buka aplikasi dan pastikan Anda sudah masuk ke akun Anda.

Launch the app and make sure you are signed in to your account.

2

Buka Profil

Go to Profile

Ketuk tab Profil di bilah navigasi bawah.

Tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation bar.

3

Ketuk "Hapus Akun"

Tap "Delete Account"

Gulir ke bawah untuk menemukan opsi Hapus Akun di bagian Data.

Scroll down to find the Delete Account option in the Data section.

4

Konfirmasi penghapusan

Confirm deletion

Ketik DELETE di kolom konfirmasi dan ketuk Hapus Selamanya.

Type DELETE in the confirmation field and tap Delete Forever.

Peringatan: Warning: Penghapusan akun bersifat permanen dan tidak dapat dibatalkan. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Data yang dihapus

What data is deleted

Ketika Anda menghapus akun, data berikut akan dihapus secara permanen:

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Profil pengguna Anda (nama, email, nomor telepon)
  • Your user profile (name, email, phone number)
  • Semua kendaraan terdaftar dan detailnya
  • All registered vehicles and their details
  • Semua catatan dan riwayat pembayaran pajak
  • All tax payment records and history
  • Semua pengaturan dan jadwal notifikasi
  • All notification settings and schedules
  • Kredensial autentikasi Anda
  • Your authentication credentials

Penyimpanan data

Data retention

Semua data dihapus segera setelah penghapusan akun. Tidak ada masa pemulihan — setelah dihapus, data Anda tidak dapat dipulihkan.

All data is deleted immediately upon account deletion. There is no recovery period — once deleted, your data cannot be restored.
